About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Virginia Tech. I am a member of the Hybrid Dynamic Systems and Robot Locomotion Lab, advised by Kaveh Akbari Hamed. I completed my M.S. in Transdisciplinary Studies at Seoul National University. I was a member of the Dynamic Robotic Systems Lab, advised by Jaeheung Park. I did my B.S. in ME at Seoul National University.
