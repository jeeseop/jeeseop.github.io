About me

I am a postdoc at Caltech. I am currently working in the AMBER Lab with Prof. Aaron Ames. I received my B.S. in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University, and my M.S. at Seoul National University supervised by Prof. Jaeheung Park. I completed my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering at Virginia Tech, advised by Prof. Kaveh Akbari Hamed.

Research Interests

  • Safety-Critical Collaborative robotics

  • Reasoning about Uncertainty and Safety

  • Bipedal/Quadrupedal locomotion

  • Hybrid dynamical systems

  • Underactuated systems

  • Decentralized/Distributed control

  • Optimization
