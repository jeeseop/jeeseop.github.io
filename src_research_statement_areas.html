Research Statement

My research goal is to establish a firm foundation that will extend the state-of-the-art methods for designing resilient and intelligent control algorithms for a wide range of legged robot applications. This overview includes 1) collaborative multi-agent systems with decentralized and distributed control policies, 2) legged robots with safety-critical control, 3) trajectory optimization and path planning for various applications on autonomous robots.
