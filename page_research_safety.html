Research Statement

My research goal is to establish a firm foundation that will extend the state-of-the-art methods for designing resilient and intelligent control algorithms for a wide range of collaborative work. This overview includes 1) collaborative multi-agent systems with decentralized and distributed control policies, 2) autonomous robots and planning for various applications, 3) agile robots without compromising safety features.
